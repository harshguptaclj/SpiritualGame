About the Game

The modes of material nature, also known as the three gunas, are fundamental qualities or energies that are believed to pervade
the material world according to various philosophical and spiritual traditions, including Hinduism and the Bhagavad Gita. These
modes influence and shape the behavior, mindset, and consciousness of living beings. The three modes of material nature are:

Mode of Goodness(sattva)

Mode of Passion(rajas)

Mode of Ignorance(tamas)

Each mode is characterised by certain qualities and tendencies. This game will check these qualities in you by asking some questions.
You just have to rate yourself on the scale of 10 on those qualities. Internal algorithm will tell you that in which mode you are in currently.

Note: These modes are not fixed categories or personality types, but rather tendencies that influence human behavior.
Individuals may exhibit qualities from different modes at different times and can work towards transcending the modes
through spiritual practices and self-awareness.
